AR Rahman

We welcome not just a musician, but a phenomenon — a name that has become synonymous with musical brilliance, spiritual depth, and global harmony. A.R. Rahman, the “Mozart of Madras,” is a composer, performer, and visionary whose music has redefined the soundscape of Indian and world cinema.

From the soul-stirring strains of Roja and Dil Se, to the Oscar-winning anthem Jai Ho, Rahman’s melodies have transcended language and geography, touching hearts across continents. His music doesn’t just entertain — it elevates. It speaks of love, longing, peace, and the divine, all woven into intricate soundscapes that blend classical traditions with contemporary rhythms.

With over three decades of groundbreaking work, countless global honours, and a legacy that continues to inspire new generations, A.R. Rahman stands not only as a master of music, but as a beacon of artistic integrity and human connection.

Prepare to be moved, uplifted, and transformed —
As we welcome the legend himself: A.R. Rahman.
